A browser-based script — paste it in your console while logged in to x.com. No installs, no credentials, no server.

📋

Copy the code

Click the COPY button below to copy the script to your clipboard.

🌐

Go to x.com

Open x.com in your browser and make sure you're logged in to your account.

🛠️

Open the console

Press Ctrl+Shift+J on Windows or ⌘+⌥+J on Mac to open DevTools.

▶️

Paste & run

Paste the code in the console and press Enter. Click SCAN in the panel that appears.

⚠️
Privacy notice: This script runs entirely in your browser and communicates only with X's own servers (api.twitter.com) using your existing login session. No data is sent anywhere else. Your credentials are never touched by this tool.
